DAVID YAYO

As a multi-talented human, I have had to struggle with my talents.

Be that as it may, I find this an opportunity to let you know who I am and what I am about.

If I wasn’t in the studio, I would be on my laptop learning code for the love of it. There, the struggle between what I had control over and what I did not have control over, came. I have a lot to share when it comes to businesses and how or why they spend time working and not achieving results.
