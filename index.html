MEET

DAVID YAYO

As a multi-talented human, I have had to struggle with my talents.

Be that as it may, I find this an opportunity to let you know who I am and what I am about.

Started as a recording and performing artiste sometime in 2013, having collaborated with Nigeria’s and Africa’s A-list acts such as Vector Tha Viper, Khaligraph Jones, and Muno; I believe when I eventually made my switch to tech, this informed me better on how to interface with clients without developing cold feet.

If I wasn’t in the studio, I would be on my laptop learning code for the love of it. There, the struggle between what I had control over and what I did not have control over, came.

With tech, I have full control over my life and I love it this way. Music is a hobby now.

If you made it here, you probably are interested in me a little.

With my little time in the tech space, I have figured, the development of a design or the design of the development will be unproductive if the team lead is lost in some areas.

It takes a lot of guts to tell your lead that whatever techniques or concepts they are using are off the scope or maybe, even a team player is causing a leak on the ship. However, for you to spot this crack on the ship you must be well experienced too.

I have a lot to share when it comes to businesses and how or why they spend time working and not achieving results. After hiring a bunch of inexperienced people who don’t share the same vision as the organization.

I am a lover of inbound.
